About ElectroVote

Trusted digital voting built for campus life

Paper ballots are slow, costly and hard to audit. ElectroVote brings elections online with bank-grade session security, automatic duplicate-vote prevention and instant, tamper-evident results — so student governance is fair, fast and fully transparent.

  • ✓ Encrypted, signed login sessions for every voter
  • ✓ Hard guarantee of one ballot per registered student
  • ✓ Real-time results the moment polls close
  • ✓ Effortless administration for the electoral committee

🔐

Secure authentication

Hashed passwords and signed session cookies keep every student and admin account protected from end to end.

🛡️

One person, one vote

A database-level guarantee prevents duplicate ballots, so results always reflect the true will of voters.

🗳️

Candidate management

Admins add candidates with positions and manifestos that appear as clean, responsive cards on the ballot.

📊

Real-time results

Watch results update live with clear progress bars and instant percentages the moment votes come in.

⚙️

Election control

Responsive by design
